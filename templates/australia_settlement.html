General Skilled Migration (GSM)

Secure Your Future Down Under

Australia offers an unmatched combination of high earning potential, excellent work-life balance, and spectacular landscapes. Through the SkillSelect points-based system, professionals can build a highly rewarding career and secure permanent residency for their entire family.

Top PR Pathways

1. Skilled Independent (Subclass 189)

The most sought-after PR visa. No sponsor or state nomination is required. If your occupation is on the MLTSSL and you score enough points on your EOI, you can live and work anywhere in Australia.

2. Skilled Nominated (Subclass 190)

If your occupation is in demand in a specific Australian state, they can nominate you. This grants an extra 5 PR points and allows you to settle in that specific region.

3. Employer Sponsored (TSS 482 / 186)

For professionals who have a concrete job offer from an approved Australian employer. This can act as a direct route to PR or a transition pathway after working for a few years.

Minimum Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Limit: Must be under 45 years of age at the time of invitation.
  • Occupation: Your profession must be listed on the relevant Skilled Occupation List.
  • Points Test: Score a minimum of 65 points (based on age, education, experience, etc.).
  • English Proficiency: Minimum Competent English (IELTS 6.0/PTE 50 in each band).

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I include my family in my PR application?

Yes! The Subclass 189 and 190 visas allow you to include your spouse/de facto partner and dependent children in the same application, granting them full PR status as well.

How long does the process take?

Processing times vary based on the occupation and points score, but typically range from 8 to 14 months from the start of the Skill Assessment to the final Visa Grant.
