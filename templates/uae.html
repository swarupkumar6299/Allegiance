Top UAE Residency Pathways

The UAE has modernized its immigration system, moving beyond the traditional employer-sponsored model to attract top global talent. We help you navigate these premium routes:

1. The 10-Year Golden Visa

The ultimate self-sponsored residency. Available to highly skilled professionals (earning AED 30,000+ per month), investors, doctors, scientists, and outstanding students. It grants you and your family 10 years of renewable residency independent of any employer.

2. The 5-Year Green Visa

A flexible, self-sponsored 5-year residency aimed at freelancers, self-employed individuals, and skilled employees (bachelor's degree required, minimum salary of AED 15,000). It allows you to easily sponsor family members with extended grace periods.

3. Standard Employment Visa (Mainland & Free Zone)

The traditional 2-year residency sponsored by your UAE employer. We assist professionals in ensuring their contracts are properly registered with MOHRE or the relevant Free Zone authority.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a sponsor to get the Golden Visa?

No! The Golden Visa and Green Visa are self-sponsored. This means your residency is not tied to a specific employer, giving you complete freedom to change jobs or start a business without risking your visa status.

What is the difference between Mainland and Free Zone?

A Mainland company is registered with the Department of Economic Development (DED) and can do business anywhere in the UAE. A Free Zone company operates within designated areas (like DIFC or DMCC) and offers 100% foreign ownership and specialized tax benefits, but is restricted to doing business within that zone or internationally.

What is an Emirates ID?

The Emirates ID is an identity card issued by the Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs and Port Security (ICP). It is a legal requirement for all citizens and residents and is used to access government services, open bank accounts, and rent property.
