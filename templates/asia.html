Top Asian Residency Pathways

Whether you are looking to relocate your business, invest in premium real estate, or enjoy a luxurious retirement, Asia offers tailored, fast-tracked visas:

1. UAE (Dubai) Golden Visa

A highly coveted 10-year renewable residency visa designed for investors, entrepreneurs, specialized talents, and outstanding students. Enjoy a 0% personal income tax environment and one of the safest luxury lifestyles in the world.

2. Singapore Global Investor Program (GIP) & EntrePass

Singapore is the premier wealth hub of Asia. The GIP grants Permanent Residency to eligible global investors who intend to drive their businesses and investment growth from Singapore. The EntrePass is ideal for serial tech entrepreneurs.

3. Malaysia (MM2H) & Thailand Elite

Premium, long-term visa programs perfect for digital nomads, retirees, and lifestyle investors. These programs offer 5 to 20-year multiple-entry visas, VIP fast-track airport services, and exceptional cost-of-living advantages.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who qualifies for the UAE Golden Visa?

The 10-year Golden Visa is available to real estate investors (minimum investment of AED 2 million), entrepreneurs with approved projects, highly skilled professionals (doctors, engineers, IT experts) with a high salary bracket, and outstanding students/graduates.

Does Asian residency lead to citizenship?

Generally, Asian nations are very protective of their citizenship. While Singapore offers a clear (but strict) path from Permanent Residency to Citizenship, countries like the UAE and Thailand primarily offer long-term, renewable residency visas rather than passports.

Can I bring my family?

Yes. Programs like the UAE Golden Visa, Singapore GIP, and Malaysia MM2H are designed with families in mind, allowing you to sponsor your spouse, children, and in some cases, your parents and domestic staff.
