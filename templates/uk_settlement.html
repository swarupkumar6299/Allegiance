Points-Based Immigration

Advance Your Career in the UK

The United Kingdom is a global hub for business, finance, healthcare, and technology. Under the UK's Points-Based Immigration System, skilled professionals with a valid job offer from an approved employer can fast-track their move and enjoy a high standard of living.

The 70-Point Requirement

1. Valid Job Offer & CoS (50 Points)

You automatically get 50 mandatory points if you have a job offer and a Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) from an approved UK sponsor at the required skill level, and pass the English language requirement.

2. Salary Threshold (20 Points)

The remaining 20 points are earned if your job pays the required minimum salary, or if your occupation is on the Immigration Salary List (e.g., healthcare, engineering).

3. Global Talent Visa

An alternative, highly prestigious route for recognized leaders in academia, research, arts, or digital technology. This visa does not require a job offer and allows maximum flexibility.

Why Professionals Choose the UK

NHS Access

Full access to the world-renowned National Health Service (NHS) for you and your family.

Pathway to ILR

Apply for Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR) after 5 continuous years of working.
