Top Immigration Pathways

We specialize in navigating the complexities of Canadian immigration. Based on your work experience and profile, we will target the fastest route to your Permanent Residency (PR).

1. Express Entry System

The fastest and most popular pathway. It manages applications for the Federal Skilled Worker Program (FSWP), Federal Skilled Trades Program (FSTP), and Canadian Experience Class (CEC). We help optimize your Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) score to secure your Invitation to Apply (ITA).

2. Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP)

Provinces like Ontario (OINP), British Columbia (BC PNP), and Alberta nominate candidates based on local labor market needs. A provincial nomination practically guarantees your PR by adding 600 points to your Express Entry profile.

3. Global Talent Stream (Tech Workers)

An expedited work permit process designed for highly skilled tech professionals, allowing you to start working in Canada in as little as two weeks while your PR processes.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Express Entry process take?

Once your profile is submitted and you receive an Invitation to Apply (ITA), the standard processing time by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) is typically 6 to 8 months.

Do I need a job offer to get Canada PR?

No, a job offer is not mandatory for the Federal Skilled Worker Program under Express Entry. However, having a valid job offer can significantly boost your CRS score and fast-track your application.

Can I include my family in my PR application?

Yes! You can include your spouse or common-law partner and dependent children under the age of 22 in your permanent residency application.
